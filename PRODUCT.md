# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Cinephiles, casual viewers, and movie enthusiasts seeking a premium platform to explore media trends, curate personal watchlists, and enjoy a high-fidelity cinematic catalog experience in both English and Arabic (supporting native RTL).

## Product Purpose
A premium, highly responsive entertainment hub integrated with the TMDb API that helps users discover cinematic media, view rich details, manage customized watchlists, and subscribe to plans—all wrapped in a highly immersive web app interface with desktop-grade animations and localization.

## Brand Personality
*Cinematic, Premium, Immersive*. Deep, dark theatre-inspired colors, fluid layout motions, strong typographic hierarchies, and rich visual presentation that focuses attention entirely on the art of cinema.

## Anti-references
*   **Sterile SaaS Interfaces**: No generic corporate layouts, outline-heavy cards, or blue-on-white gradients.
*   **Stark Light Backgrounds**: Avoid dazzling light modes that break the dim, eye-friendly theater environment.
*   **Flat & Lifeless Grids**: No boring, non-interactive catalog cards that feel like a file system rather than a movie gallery.
*   **Broken RTL Layouts**: Avoid lazy Arabic alignment shifts or un-optimized font scaling between Arabic and English text.

## Design Principles
1. **The Screen is Your Canvas**: Place cinematic media at the center. Use rich, full-bleed backdrops, dynamic posters, and atmospheric overlays to pull the user in.
2. **Fluidity Over Staticity**: Keep the experience alive. Use smooth carousel sweeps, micro-interactions on hover, and seamless transition states to make browsing feel tactile and responsive.
3. **Symmetric Bilingual Harmony**: Balance English (LTR) and Arabic (RTL) seamlessly. Ensure typography scales beautifully in both languages and layouts mirror perfectly without compromising aesthetic balance.
4. **Focused Simplicity**: Keep control buttons, watchlist actions, and plan options accessible yet quiet, allowing the movie poster art and trailers to dominate the visual hierarchy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
*   Targeting high-contrast dark mode designs with optimized visual comfort for night-time viewing.
*   Ensuring red accents and secondary text hold a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 against absolute and tinted dark backdrops.
*   Maintaining strict touch target sizes (minimum 44px) across all interactive media controls, Swiper arrows, and card buttons.
*   Pairing high-readability sans-serif fonts to support both Latin characters and native Arabic scripts gracefully.
